# Travelog Planner

Plan a trip as a chronological sequence of places and the routes between them.

## Start here

1. Create a trip in **Trips**.
2. Create one row per date in **Days** and relate each day to its trip.
3. Add both places and routes to **Timeline**.
4. Sort Timeline by `Current start`, then `Sort order`.
5. Before departure, copy current times into baseline times to preserve the
   original plan.

## Planning dashboard

Add linked views here after importing the databases:

- **Upcoming trips:** Trips filtered to `Planning` or `Ready`.
- **Selected trip days:** Days grouped by Trip and sorted by Date.
- **Daily timeline:** Timeline grouped by Day, sorted by Current start.
- **Open checklist:** Checklist filtered to Completed is unchecked.
- **Planned budget:** Expenses grouped by Trip and Currency, sum Amount.

## Data promise

Your stable IDs and raw planning data are designed to be portable to the future
Travelog service. The free template remains useful without a Travelog account.
